    About Insomnia

    "Insomnia" is an auditory journey through the sleepless hours, a soundscape painted with the quiet desperation and delicate beauty of the night. Each track is a vignette, a fleeting thought in the dark, exploring themes of isolation, introspection, and the surreal clarity that only comes when the world is asleep.
